Thesis

I spent 15 years across data analytics, data science, and data engineering at Snowflake, LinkedIn, TripAdvisor, and others before becoming an operator and franchise owner. I understand both sides of the ledger. The firm I am acquiring keeps everything that makes a great accounting practice great: trusted staff, long client relationships, deep compliance work. And it adds what most CPA firms simply cannot offer: real technical infrastructure. Automated reporting. System integrations. A consolidated financial picture for business owners and individuals whose complexity has outgrown what a standard accountant can handle. The clients who need this know exactly who they are. They have asked their CPA for it and been told it is not possible. We are the answer to that question.

Target Firm Criteria

SDE $300K - $500K+
Valuation Range $750K - $2.5M
Geography Sacramento Metro / East Bay / Fully Virtual
Services Bookkeeping, tax, compliance (recurring revenue base)
Client Mix SMB businesses + HNW individuals with multi-asset complexity
Staff Requirement Team owns client relationships directly, not routed through the owner
Technology Cloud-based stack preferred (QBO Online, Xero, Karbon, TaxDome)
The Signal Clients have requested reporting, dashboards, or integrations the current owner could not provide

Deal Structure

Seller Financing Preferred SBA 7(a) Available Earnout / Rollover Equity OK LOI Target: June 2026 Close Target: Q3 2026

Seller financing preferred, structured around client retention through transition. Open to SBA 7(a) where deal size warrants it. Earnout or rollover equity welcomed where the seller wants continued participation. Pre-qualified and ready to move quickly when the right firm surfaces.

Buyer Type

Individual buyer deploying personal capital. Hands-on operator, not a financial engineer. Focused on learning the craft, supporting the team, and growing the business.

Post-Close Commitment

The staff stays. The brand stays. The compliance work continues exactly as clients expect it. What changes is that the firm gains a technical capability it did not have before, available to clients who want it, on their timeline, without disrupting anything that is already working.

This is not an absorption play. It is not an offshoring play. It is a build, adding a layer on top of a stable foundation that the seller spent years creating.
